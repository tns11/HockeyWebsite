Indian Hockey

Tournaments



Hockey is the national game of India and Indian team had once been the undisputed champion of the game. Several Indian and international tournaments have been held in the Indian hockey stadiums in past and during its hay days, Indian domestic tournaments of Hockey were of equal standards to the world class tournaments. Even today, the Indian hockey tournaments are played throughout the year and are conducted under the aegis of the Indian Hockey federation. Most of them are named after the wizards of the game or some renowned personality in the country. Here is a list of all the national hockey competitions in India.
